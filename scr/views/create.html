Welcome

Welcome to GameCampus: Hero Forger
Are you ready to create the ultimate hero?

Step into the heart of an epic fantasy world where you are the architect of destiny. Become the creator of legends in a unique platform inspired by the universe of Dungeons & Dragons.
🛡️ What is GameCampus?

GameCampus gives you the power to design fully customized characters with an immersive visual experience.
Choose everything from their race to their special abilities and equip your champion with the finest in fantasy weaponry.
Then, save your creation to revisit or share it whenever you like.

⚔️ Main Features

Create unique characters from scratch by choosing:

🧙 A custom name for your hero or heroine.
🧝‍♂️ Race: Choose from humans, elves, orcs, dwarves, and more (via our fantasy races API).
🗡️ Class: Be a warrior, mage, rogue, cleric, and many more (from the classes API).
⚧️ Gender: Male, Female, or Custom.
🛡️ Armor and Weapons: Use our arsenal or create your own.
💪 Custom Stats: Define strength, intelligence, dexterity, wisdom, charisma, and constitution.
🔮 Special Abilities: Magic, powers, and unique talents from our universe.
💍 Legendary Accessories: Rings, pendants, enchanted cloaks, and more.

💾 Save Your Hero

Finished your masterpiece? Save it! You’ll be able to view it in your personal gallery, edit it, or share it with other adventurers.

📜 Character View

Explore a complete list of characters filtered by your choices, with the option to view full details, stats, and equipment.

📱 Compatible with All Your Devices
The platform is designed to provide a smooth experience on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers. Your hero will always be within reach.
